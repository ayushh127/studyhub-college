# Future AI Features

While the MVP of StudyHub College does not include AI components, the architecture is designed to support them in the future.

## Planned AI Modules

1. **PDF to Quiz Generation:**
   - Upload a PDF -> Extract text -> Automatically generate MCQs.
   - **Crucial Workflow:** Generated questions must go into a "Pending Review" state. The college admin must review, edit, and manually publish them. We will never publish AI-generated content automatically.

2. **AI-Generated Content:**
   - Generating explanations for difficult topics based on the uploaded syllabus.

3. **Student AI Tutor:**
   - An interactive tutor that can answer student questions strictly based on the college's uploaded PDFs and PYQs.
   - Includes AI concept explanation and doubt solving capabilities.

4. **Subscription & Monetization:**
   - Premium AI features may be gated behind a subscription model in the future.

5. **Local vs Cloud Processing:**
   - Support for local LLM integrations (like Ollama) during development to save costs.
   - Cloud AI provider integration for production scale.
