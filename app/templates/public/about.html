About StudyHub

StudyHub College is a dedicated digital workspace designed to bridge the gap between academic institutions and their students. Our mission is to provide a serene, distraction-free environment for rigorous academic preparation and peer resource sharing.

Our Philosophy

We believe that learning tools should not distract. Adopting a Modern Minimal Academic aesthetic, StudyHub focuses on clarity and focus—reducing cognitive load so students can immerse themselves in their studies.

Core Features (MVP)

  • College-Specific Curricula: Materials strictly mapped to your college's official syllabus and course structure.
  • Interactive Quiz Engine: Practice concepts and prepare for exams with customized interactive test questions.
  • Official PYQs & Slides: Centralized access to verified Lecture Notes and Previous Year Question papers.
  • Community Library: Share your notes & PDF papers to help others.
